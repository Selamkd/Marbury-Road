Marbury Road Edible Garden

Born out of a need for food security in Stockport, Madbury garden has cultivated a space for the local community to learn, to grow and harvest crops freely!

JOIN US

Marbury Road Edible Garden is a volunteer-run community group focusing on gardening and growing food for the community. It also runs wellbeing workshops, including yoga, mindful watercolour painting, sound and healing meditation, and more!

For the Community by the Community

We're not just about growing plants – we're also committed to nurturing a sustainable ecosystem. Our initiative involves partnering with local restaurants and households to rescue organic waste that often ends up in landfills. By cycling this waste back to our garden, we create nutrient-rich compost that fosters healthy plant growth. Our dedicated collection days on Mondays and Wednesdays, from 10am to 6pm, ensure that every contribution counts toward a greener environment.
